About Radicle

Radicle's mission is to revolutionize the software industry

We feel deeply the potential for technology to transform people's lives in a positive way. It's really exciting to look around and see truly awful software everywhere. What an impact can be made!

Some of the big software companies, like Google and Apple, are starting to get serious about app design; their activity in this area is going to put pressure on all software to behave better.

We want to help everyone reap the fruits of the knowledge being created. We want small and medium enterprise, as well as end consumers, to be empowered by having astonishingly more effective and elegant interactions with technology.
